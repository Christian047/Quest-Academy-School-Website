Admissions

Application Form:

Obtain the application form from the school office or download it here

Submission of Application:
Complete the application form and submit it along with the required documents: Birth certificate Recent passport-sized photographs Previous school records (if applicable) Immunization records Entrance Examination:

Entrance Exams
All prospective students must sit for an entrance examination, which assesses basic literacy and numeracy skills. Exam dates will be communicated via the school’s website and notice boards. Interview:

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with the admissions committee. This may include a brief assessment of the child's interests and abilities. Admission Offer:

Admission Offer
Successful candidates will receive an admission offer via email or letter. Parents must confirm acceptance of the offer within the specified timeframe. Registration:

Step 1:
Complete the Application Form Log in to your account and fill out the form here with the required information: Student’s full name Date of birth Previous school (if applicable) Parent/Guardian details

Step 2:
Upload Required Documents Scan and upload the following documents: Birth certificate Recent passport-sized photographs Previous school records (if applicable) Immunization records

Step 3:
Entrance Examination Registration Choose a preferred date for the entrance examination from the available slots. Confirmation of your chosen date will be sent to your registered email.

Step 4:
Payment of Application Fee Pay the non-refundable application fee via the secure payment portal. Keep the payment receipt as proof of transaction.
