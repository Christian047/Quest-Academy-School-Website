Raising role models...
At Quest Academy, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom; it is about nurturing character, integrity, and leadership in our students. Our motto, "Raising Role Models," encapsulates our commitment to shaping young minds into responsible, compassionate individuals who will positively impact their communities and the world.
Your children will have all the basic necessities for a holistic education, including a nurturing environment, access to quality resources, and comprehensive support systems. At Quest Academy, we focus on the development of the whole child—intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically.

Entrance Exams
All prospective students must sit for an entrance examination, which assesses basic literacy and numeracy skills. Exam dates will be communicated via the school’s website and notice boards. Interview:

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with the admissions committee. This may include a brief assessment of the child's interests and abilities. Admission Offer:

Admission Offer
Successful candidates will receive an admission offer via email or letter. Parents must confirm acceptance of the offer within the specified timeframe. Registration:

School Enquiries

School Calendar for Quest Academy Primary School, Lagos:

The Quest Academy School Calendar is a comprehensive guide designed to keep parents, students, and staff informed about key dates and events throughout the academic year.

By following this calendar, families can better plan their schedules and ensure that they don’t miss important school activities, including holidays, examinations, and parent-teacher interactions. It serves as a roadmap for a successful academic journey at Quest Academy.

Term Start Dates:

The first term of the academic year will commence on September 1st. In preparation for this exciting new school year, we will hold an orientation session on August 30th for new students and their families.

During this event, parents will have the opportunity to meet with teachers, tour the school facilities, and familiarize themselves with the curriculum and school policies. This session aims to ease the transition for new students and ensure they feel welcomed and supported as they start their educational journey at Quest Academy.

Newsletter for Quest Academy Primary School, Lagos:

The Quest Academy newsletter is a valuable resource for keeping parents, students, and staff informed about school activities, achievements, and upcoming events. It serves as a platform for sharing important announcements and highlights from the school community.

Monthly Updates:

Each month, our newsletter features updates on various school activities, including sports events, art exhibitions, and academic achievements. Parents can expect to find highlights of student accomplishments and stories that celebrate the vibrant life at Quest Academy.

Important Announcements:

Our newsletter also includes crucial announcements regarding deadlines, events, and any changes to the school schedule. This ensures that parents are always in the loop and can plan accordingly for their children’s education.

Parent Involvement:

We encourage parents to get involved by reading the newsletter regularly. It contains information about volunteer opportunities, parent workshops, and ways to engage with the school community.

E-Learning at Quest Academy Primary School, Lagos:

Quest Academy’s e-learning platform is designed to enhance the educational experience for our students. It provides access to a wide range of resources and interactive tools that support learning both in and out of the classroom.

Online Resources:

Our e-learning platform offers a variety of online resources, including educational videos, interactive quizzes, and downloadable materials. These resources are tailored to complement the curriculum and help reinforce key concepts.

Virtual Classrooms:

In addition to resources, the platform features virtual classrooms where teachers can conduct live lessons and discussions. This fosters engagement and allows students to interact with their peers and instructors in real-time.

Accessibility and Support:

Our e-learning platform is accessible from any device with internet connectivity, ensuring that students can learn anytime and anywhere. We also provide technical support for families to help navigate the platform effectively.
